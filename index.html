Junchi Yu

I am a 4th-year Ph.D. student at Insititute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences, advised by Prof. Ran He.

Previouly, I was a research intern at Tencent AI LAB, working with Dr. Tingyang Xu, Dr. Yu Rong, Dr. Yatao Bian and Prof. Junzhou Huang.

I aim to develop Trustworthy Graph Learning (TwGL) methods with good interpretability and transferbility, and their interdiscinplinary applications such as drug design and protein-ligand binding.
